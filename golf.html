GOLF

"The ardent golfer would play Mount Everest if somebody put a flagstick on top." - Pete Dye

Imagine a sport in which the elements dictate everything and you will stand alone on a Sunday. This sport has been a staple in my life for the past 23 years. It's the closets game to life, you will get bad breaks in great positions, and good breaks in bad positions. It is a sport where the only person to blame for failure is yourself. It'll leave you scratching your head but wanting more than ever before. Its a constant chase for perfection that you will never catch. This is the amazing game of golf.
